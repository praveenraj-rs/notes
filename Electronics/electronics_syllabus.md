### **Electronics Study Tree Structure**

#### **1. Basics of Electronics**
   - **1.1 Fundamental Concepts**
     - Voltage, Current, Resistance, and Power
     - Ohm's Law and Kirchhoff's Laws
     - Electrical Components: Resistors, Capacitors, Inductors
     - Energy Sources: DC and AC Supplies
   - **1.2 Circuit Analysis**
     - Series and Parallel Circuits
     - Thevenin's and Norton's Theorems
     - Mesh and Nodal Analysis
     - Impedance and Reactance

#### **2. Analog Electronics**
   - **2.1 Semiconductor Basics**
     - Band Theory and Conductors/Insulators
     - P-N Junctions and Diodes
   - **2.2 Diodes**
     - Types of Diodes: Zener, LED, Schottky, Photodiodes
     - Applications: Rectifiers, Voltage Regulators, Clipping and Clamping Circuits
   - **2.3 Bipolar Junction Transistors (BJTs)**
     - Transistor Operation and Configurations (CE, CB, CC)
     - Amplifiers: Class A, B, AB, C
     - Biasing Techniques
   - **2.4 Field Effect Transistors (FETs)**
     - JFET and MOSFET Operation
     - Applications in Switching and Amplification
   - **2.5 Operational Amplifiers (Op-Amps)**
     - Inverting and Non-Inverting Amplifiers
     - Applications: Filters, Oscillators, Comparators, and Integrators
   - **2.6 Power Electronics**
     - SCR, TRIAC, DIAC, and UJTs
     - AC-DC Converters: Rectifiers, Inverters, and Choppers
   - **2.7 Signal Processing**
     - Sinusoidal and Non-Sinusoidal Waveforms
     - Filters: Low-Pass, High-Pass, Band-Pass, Band-Stop
     - Modulation and Demodulation (AM, FM)

#### **3. Digital Electronics**
   - **3.1 Number Systems and Codes**
     - Binary, Octal, Decimal, Hexadecimal
     - Binary Arithmetic and Logic
     - BCD, Gray Code, ASCII
   - **3.2 Logic Gates and Boolean Algebra**
     - Basic Gates: AND, OR, NOT, XOR, XNOR
     - Universal Gates: NAND, NOR
     - Boolean Algebra and Simplification
   - **3.3 Combinational Logic Circuits**
     - Multiplexers and Demultiplexers
     - Encoders and Decoders
     - Adders, Subtractors, and Comparators
   - **3.4 Sequential Logic Circuits**
     - Flip-Flops, Latch: SR, JK, D, T
     - Counters: Asynchronous and Synchronous
     - Shift Registers
   - **3.5 Microprocessors and Microcontrollers**
     - Architecture and Operation of 8085/8086
     - Memory Organization and Addressing Modes
     - Microcontroller Basics: Arduino, PIC, ARM
   - **3.6 Programmable Logic Devices**
     - FPGA and CPLD Basics
     - Applications in Hardware Design
   - **3.7 Digital Communication**
     - Sampling and Quantization
     - Digital Modulation Techniques (ASK, FSK, PSK)
     - Error Detection and Correction (Hamming Code)

#### **4. Practical Applications**
   - **4.1 Circuit Design**
     - Breadboard Prototyping and Soldering
     - PCB Design and Fabrication
   - **4.2 Simulation Tools**
     - Software: SPICE, Multisim, Proteus
   - **4.3 Testing and Debugging**
     - Multimeter and Oscilloscope Usage
     - Signal Analysis and Debugging Techniques

#### **5. Advanced Topics (Optional)**
   - **5.1 VLSI Design**
     - CMOS Technology
     - Layout Design Rules and Fabrication
   - **5.2 Embedded Systems**
     - Sensors, Actuators, and Interfacing
     - Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS)
   - **5.3 Internet of Things (IoT)**
     - Basics of IoT Hardware
     - Communication Protocols (MQTT, HTTP, LoRa)
   - **5.4 Robotics and Automation**
     - Control Systems and Motor Drivers
     - Robotics Programming Platforms (ROS)
